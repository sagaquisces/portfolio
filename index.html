About me

Originally from Pasadena, California, my first passion was music. I learned how to play the viola and piano at an early age and later developed as a bass-baritone, becoming an opera singer.

My web programming interests developed after my first college experience in MIS (Management Information Systems) as I was embarking on a music degree in composition. I found I had the same thrill of creation from building a website as a I did for writing a piece.
